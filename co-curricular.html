Developing Talents, Character, and Life Skills Beyond the Classroom

Co-Curricular Integration at MCK Kariokor

At MCK Kariokor, we recognize that education is most meaningful when learners are nurtured academically, physically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually. In line with the Competency-Based Education (CBE) framework and guided by Christian values, our co-curricular programme is designed to complement classroom learning by developing talents, character, and life skills.

Through carefully planned activities, learners are encouraged to discover their God-given abilities, build confidence, and learn the values of teamwork, responsibility, and service.

Integration with the CBE Curriculum

Our co-curricular activities are fully integrated into the Competency-Based Education curriculum and are designed to develop key competencies such as communication and collaboration, critical thinking and creativity, leadership and citizenship, as well as self-management and responsibility. Assessment within these activities emphasizes participation, personal growth, effort, and skill development, ensuring that every learner's progress is recognized, nurtured, and valued.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and Christ-centered environment where learners can explore their interests, develop talents, and grow into responsible individuals. Through co-curricular integration, we prepare learners to serve God, their families, and society with confidence, integrity, and excellence.

We pride ourselves in being a leading institution of learning and offering holistic growth.
