Our Story

School History and Overview

About Us

MCK Kariokor Primary School was started in 1986 by the church's women fellowship. It started as a nursery school under the chairmanship of Mrs Hellen Mung'athia who at times doubled as a part time teacher.

It grew to produce its first pupils in 1988 and continued as a kindergarten up to 2005 when when it grew to a full nursery and primary school and had its first standard one with 9 pupils. The population continued growing gradually.

The first standard 8 did their KCPE in 2012, a few years before the school was registered as an examination center by Kenya National Examination Council.

School Projection

The school is destined for greater heights especially through structural and infrastructural face lifts brought by the hard working church management, school BOM, teachers, staff and pupils. Together we are aiming to expand the school's scope in all ways by improving all standards and especially academics. We are planning to improve our enrolment to 500 pupils. This will make MCK Kariokor a learning center of excellence in Nairobi.

May God bless MCK Kariokor School Primary and Junior Secondary.

We pride ourselves in being a leading institution of learning and offering holistic growth.
