Message from the Superintendent Minister

It is my pleasure to welcome you to MCK Kariokor Primary and Junior Secondary School — a place where every learner is valued, inspired, and guided to achieve their full potential.

Our dedicated staff and supportive community work hand in hand to create a learning environment that is both academically rigorous and morally grounded. We believe in nurturing learners who not only excel in studies but also demonstrate integrity, empathy, and resilience.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to innovation in education and to fostering the talents of every child who walks through our gates.

– The Superintendent Minister, MCK Kariokor
