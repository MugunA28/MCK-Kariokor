Curriculum Overview

Understanding Our Competency-Based Education Approach

What Is Competency-Based Education?

Competency-Based Education focuses on what learners are able to do with what they learn, rather than how much content they can memorize. Learners develop practical skills, positive attitudes, and values that prepare them for real-life situations, further education, and meaningful participation in society.

Learning is:
  • Learner-centered
  • Activity-based
  • Developmentally appropriate
  • Continuously assessed

Competency-Based Education (CBE) Curriculum Overview

MCK Kariokor Primary and Junior Secondary follows the Competency-Based Education (CBE) curriculum as guided by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD). This curriculum is designed to nurture holistic learners who are academically competent, morally upright, spiritually grounded, and socially responsible.

As a church-based school, we integrate academic excellence with Christian values, ensuring that learners grow not only in knowledge and skills, but also in character, faith, and service to others.

Our Commitment

Christian-Centered Approach

Guided by Christian principles, our school has an approach to Competency-Based Education that aims to nurture each learner's God-given talents and abilities while promoting moral integrity, discipline, and responsibility. We encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving, and we foster love, respect, and a spirit of service to God and the wider community. Through this holistic approach, we support learners to grow spiritually, emotionally, socially, and intellectually.

Quality Education for All

At our school, we are committed to providing a safe, caring, and Christ-centered learning environment while offering quality education aligned with national standards. We seek to develop confident, skilled, and God-fearing learners, and to prepare children to serve God, their families, and society with excellence.

Key Features of Our CBE Approach

Practical Skills

Learners develop real-world skills that they can apply in their daily lives and future careers.

Character Development

We foster positive attitudes and values that shape learners into responsible, caring members of society.

Critical Thinking

We emphasize problem-solving and creativity, encouraging learners to think independently and innovatively.

We pride ourselves in being a leading institution of learning and offering holistic growth.
