School Sections/Levels

Structured Learning for Every Stage of Development

Pre-Primary

Pre-Primary Education (Play Group, Pre-Primary 1 & 2)

Focuses on building a strong spiritual and moral foundation while developing communication and early literacy skills, numeracy skills, motor coordination, and social interaction. It also nurtures creativity through play, exploration, and age-appropriate activities that support holistic growth.

Key Focus Areas: Moral development, communication, early literacy, numeracy, motor skills, social interaction, and creative expression through play.

Grades 1–3

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

Learners develop strong reading, writing, and numeracy skills while gaining an understanding of their environment. They are encouraged to express creativity through music, art, and movement, and to practice good values such as honesty, respect, and cooperation in their daily interactions.

Key Focus Areas: Reading and writing proficiency, numeracy skills, environmental understanding, creative expression, and values development.

Grades 4–6

Upper Primary (Grades 4–6)

Emphasis is placed on developing core academic competencies alongside scientific thinking and inquiry. Learners build digital literacy skills, grow in social responsibility and citizenship, and are guided to strengthen Christian values as part of their daily life and learning.

Key Focus Areas: Core academic competencies, scientific inquiry, digital literacy, social responsibility, citizenship, and Christian values integration.

Grades 7–9

Junior Secondary (Grades 7–9)

Learners are guided to apply knowledge to real-world problems while developing leadership and essential life skills. They are encouraged to explore their talents and interests and are prepared for future career pathways and a life of purposeful, service-oriented living.

Key Focus Areas: Real-world problem-solving, leadership development, life skills, talent exploration, career preparation, and purpose-driven living.

Holistic Development Throughout All Levels

Spiritual Growth

At every level, we nurture learners' spiritual development and Christian values, creating a foundation for moral integrity and faith-based living.

Academic Excellence

Each level builds upon previous learning, ensuring steady academic progress while developing critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Social Skills

We develop learners' ability to interact respectfully, collaborate effectively, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

We pride ourselves in being a leading institution of learning and offering holistic growth.
